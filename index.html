The Sneaker Exchange


Capstone Project

The Sneaker Exchange is a project that I built using VueJS as the front-end framework and Ruby On Rails on the back-end. The web application is geared towards people within the sneaker community to buy, sell and trade authentic sneakers within an area of their location.


The premise of this app came from my own frustrations when buying and selling hyped sneakers through other conventional ecommerce sites such as StockX and Goat or eBay. The web-app gives people within the sneaker community another avenue to buying and selling their favorite kicks.
